Name: Prometheus
   URL: http://prometheus-kube-prometheus-prometheus.monitoring.svc:9090
   Access: Server (default)
```

5. **Save:**
   - Scroll down
   - Click **"Save & Test"**
   - You should see:  "Data source is working"

6. **Now go back and import your dashboard:**
   - Click **"+"** → **"Import"**
   - Upload `falco-security-dashboard.json`
   - The **Prometheus dropdown will now have an option!**
   - Select **"Prometheus"**
   - Click **"Import"**
```
